#+title: One-Hand Pull-Up Exercises 

- Hold angels 0, 90, 120  5 sec each, 3 reps, 3-5 minutes rest.
- Inverse pull-ups 3-5 reps.
- Pull-ups from one angle to another 3-5 reps for each angle pair. 2-3
  reps, 3-5 min rest.
- Pull-up with prejump, 8-10 on each hand alternating, 1 minute rest.
- Pull-up with rubber. 10 each hand. 3 reps.
