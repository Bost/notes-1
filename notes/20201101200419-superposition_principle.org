#+title: Superposition principle

The net repsonse caused by few stimulus is the sum of responses for
each of them.

A function F(x) that satisfies Superposition principle is called a
linear function.

#+begin_src latex
\begin{equation}
F(x_1 + x_2) = F(x_1) + F(x_2)
\alpha F(x) = F(\alpha x)
\end{equation}
#+end_src
