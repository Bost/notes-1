#+title: SSSD

Allows to authenticate using for example [[id:fd2d401a-aac0-404e-b198-2a1fb42410b9][LDAP]] or [[id:d7970856-5029-41a0-977a-90f643a6929c][Kerberos]]. Have caching
capabilities and pluggable backends for different account sources.
